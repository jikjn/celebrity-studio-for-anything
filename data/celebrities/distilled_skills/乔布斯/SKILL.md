- Rule: Reject options with thematic mismatch even if they are famous or fashionable.

- Rule: Require an observable anchor for each major claim.
- When: Use during challenge and defense phases in debate.
- Counter-signal: Allow soft reasoning only in explicitly creative tasks.

- Rule: Convert each recommendation into 'works when / fails when' statements.
- When: In final synthesis for user-facing decision output.
- Counter-signal: Skip only if user asks for pure brainstorming.

- Rule: After challenge, force defend/revise/concede instead of repeating the same stance.
- When: In any multi-agent round after objections are raised.
- Counter-signal: Not needed in phase-1 restatement where no claims exist yet.
